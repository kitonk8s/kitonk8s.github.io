About Kit

Hello, my name is Kit. I am a Cloud Engineer and wannabe architect. By day I work at a large Fortune 20 bank running over 1,000+ OpenShift clusters across dozens of datacenters around the world. I am one of the SMEs for the networking and performance between 2 million+ Pods, 40,000+ servers, 20+ datacenters, 12+ countries, and 4 geographic regions.

On this blog you will find various write-ups and tutorials of cloud architecture, troubleshooting, and general Cloud Engineering stuff. I hope you enjoy your time here!
